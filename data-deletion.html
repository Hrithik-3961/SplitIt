Data Deletion Request

At SplitIt, we value your privacy and provide you with full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by submitting a request via email.

How to delete your data:

  1. In-App (Recommended): Go to Settings > Delete Account. This will instantly and permanently wipe your profile and account from our servers.
  2. Via Email: If you no longer have the app installed or prefer to request deletion via email, please contact us at the address below.
Support Email: hrithikagarwalwork@gmail.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request - SplitIt

What data will be deleted?

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently remove the following from our database:

  • Your user profile (Name, Phone Number, UID).
  • Your individual balance records.
  • Your link to any groups you were a member of.

Note: Transaction records within a group may remain visible to other group members for accounting integrity, but your personal identity will be unlinked from them.
